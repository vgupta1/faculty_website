› About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at USC Marshall and an Affiliate Faculty at the USC Center for AI and Society. My research focuses on data-driven decisionmaking and optimization, particularly in settings where data is either scarce or high-dimensional. I am generally interested in applications in risk management, revenue management, education, healthcare and business analytics. I sometimes work as an academic consultant.

I currently serve an Associate Editor for Management Science in the ``Big Data Analytics" department (2019-present).

Before joining acadmia, I spent several years working as a Quant at Barclays Capital in commodities. I eventually left Wall Street to pursue my PhD in Operations Research at MIT. See my CV for details.
